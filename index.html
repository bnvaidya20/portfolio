About Me

I am Binod Vaidya — an applied Data Scientist and AI Specialist who turns research into production systems. Over 10+ years across academia and industry, I’ve led multi-partner projects in energy, mobility, cybersecurity, fintech, and healthcare, securing public/industry funding, and co-inventing patents in EV network authentication. As Lead Technical Specialist at BipraTek Solutions, I scope problems with stakeholders, architect end-to-end AI/ML pipelines, and deliver measurable outcomes (fraud detection, predictive maintenance, anomaly detection). I prioritize responsible and secure AI with strong governance, privacy, and sustainability practices. Beyond the technical work, I manage programs end-to-end—roadmaps, budgets, risks, and delivery—using Agile (Scrum/Kanban) and tools like Jira and Confluence to keep teams aligned and accountable.
